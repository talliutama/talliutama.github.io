WHO WE ARE

A Supplies & Trading company based in Miri, Sarawak.




We began operation in 1996, marketing full range scientific and laboratory apparatus for oil and gas and government agencies. Since then, our company has expanded our supplies to include specialized materials, marine equipments, industrial chemicals, calibration gases and many more.


We have a proven track record in providing the highest quality and safe products and services to our clients as we place great emphasis on adding value to the industry by ensuring that our products and services are reliable and safe.



Talli Utama Sdn. Bhd. is a bumiputera status company
registered and qualified by Petronas and other government agencies.

BUSINESS CATEGORIES

We act as the sole exclusive agent for some major principals in the Oil&Gas industry
and are happy to build relations with more companies in the years to come.

    Chemicals

    We are the exclusive agents for the NORCHEM line of chemicals: quality controlled chemical cleaners suited for offshore rigs, industrial and oil refineries. We also supply other chemical and reagent ranges.

    General Materials

    Safety Equipments, Instrumentations, Fire Extinguishers, O-rings, Flanges, Stud Bolts & Nuts, Valves, Body Harness, Hand Tools, Gauges, Gaskets,

    Industrial Gases

    Our Gases come complete with COA/ COC be it Calibration Gases, Purified Gases, Reference Gases or Industrial Gases.

    Marine Equips.

    Customers look for us to supply a range of marine equipment and accessories from wire ropes, mooring ropes, shackles, rope slings, scramble nets to life rafts, chains or anchors.

    Lab Supplies

    Equipments ranging from deionizers, microscopes, particle sieves, analyzers, titrators, GC, moisture analyzers, heating mantles to glasswares, ovens, and washers.

    Medical Equips.

    We know where to get the best Stretches, EEG machines, Cooling/ Heating Circulators, Examination apparatus, Resuscitation equipments and etc.
